Our Signature Fragrances

Introducing Fleur de Georgia Eau de Parfum by Jardin d’Été

Step into a world of lush elegance and Southern charm with Fleur de Georgia Eau de Parfum, the latest luxurious creation from Jardin d’Été. Crafted with care in the heart of Atlanta, Georgia, this fragrance captures the essence of the Peach State with notes that pay homage to Georgia’s state fruit, blending ripe sweetness with a sophisticated edge.

At the top, Fleur de Georgia opens with a lively burst of fresh Bergamot and cooling Peppermint Arvensis, conjuring the early-morning warmth and vibrancy of Atlanta’s blooming gardens. A touch of Pink Pepper adds a subtle hint of spice, lending an irresistible allure that intrigues and excites.

The heart is where Fleur de Georgia reveals its true, romantic depth. A luxurious floral bouquet of Masala Rose and rare Yara Yara oils creates a unique, sophisticated profile, blending beautifully with luscious Peach and green Verbena. Together, they embody a flirtatious sweetness that feels modern yet timeless, capturing the heart and soul of Georgia in bloom.

As it settles, the fragrance unfolds its rich, long-lasting base notes, with Sandalwood, Amber, and the powdery warmth of Orris Root, all softened by the sensual touch of Vanillin and creamy Cacao. This deep, lingering foundation ensures that Fleur de Georgia remains on the skin, enveloping you in an aura of sensuality and refined grace from day to night.

A Lasting Signature of Elegance and Sophistication
Designed to accompany the warm breezes of spring and summer, Fleur de Georgia Eau de Parfum leaves a lasting impression, staying vibrant on the skin for hours. Whether for a daytime garden party or an intimate evening out, its enduring, luxurious sillage turns heads, making it the perfect scent for those who seek a fragrance as unforgettable as they are.
