Introducing Fleur de Georgia Eau de Parfum

Step into a world of lush elegance and Southern charm with Fleur de Georgia Eau de Parfum, the latest luxurious creation from Jardin d’Ete LLC. Crafted with care in the heart of Atlanta, Georgia, this fragrance captures the essence of the Peach State with notes that honor its signature fruit — blending ripe sweetness with a refined, sophisticated edge.

At the top, Fleur de Georgia bursts forth with fresh Bergamot and cooling Peppermint Arvensis, evoking the early-morning warmth and vibrancy of Atlanta’s blooming gardens. A dash of Pink Pepper adds a subtle, intriguing spice, igniting a captivating allure.

The heart reveals a romantic, floral bouquet — luxurious Masala Rose and rare Yara Yara oils mingle beautifully with luscious Peach and vibrant Verbena, creating an elegant, unforgettable profile.

Finally, the fragrance settles into a rich, long-lasting base featuring creamy Sandalwood, warm Amber, and the powdery softness of Orris Root, all harmonized with hints of Vanillin and velvety Cacao.

A Lasting Signature of Elegance and Sophistication

Designed to accompany the warm breezes of spring and summer, Fleur de Georgia Eau de Parfum leaves a lasting impression, staying vibrant on the skin for hours. Whether gracing a daytime garden party or an intimate evening occasion, its enduring, luxurious sillage commands attention — perfect for those who desire a fragrance as unforgettable as they are.
